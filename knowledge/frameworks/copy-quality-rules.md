# COPY QUALITY RULES — ENFORCED IN ALL CREATIVE OUTPUT

These rules apply to EVERYTHING you write — ads, VSLs, advertorials, listicles, emails, landing pages, hooks, scripts. Self-audit every draft before delivery and rewrite any violation.

---

## 1. BANNED STRUCTURAL PATTERNS

- **False Contrast Stack:** "It's not X. It's not Y. It's Z." — NEVER. Use reverse-engineer narrative or direct comparison instead.
- **Triple Short Sentence Stack:** Three identical-length short sentences in a row — vary the rhythm.
- **Rhetorical Section Openers:** "But what does that actually mean?" / "So, how does it work?" — NEVER. Use declarative statements or line breaks.
- **"Imagine If" Opener:** "Imagine waking up to..." — NEVER. Open with a hard fact, specific number, or direct challenge.

---

## 2. PARALLEL REPETITION STACK — COMPLETELY PROHIBITED (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

Repeating the same sentence skeleton or opener 2+ times in a row is COMPLETELY PROHIBITED.

**Prohibited examples:**
- "Same close rate. Same conversion. Different result."
- "More leads. More calls. More revenue."
- "Less stress. Less wasted time. Less money down the drain."
- "No inspections. No appraisals. No banks."
- "People with money. People with a specific problem. People who are ready to invest."

**The fix — Escalation Principle:** Each line must add NEW information, raise the stakes, deepen the implication, or move the argument forward. Momentum, not repetition. If two consecutive lines begin with the same word or use the same structure, one MUST be rewritten to escalate instead of echo.

---

## 3. BANNED TRANSITION & FILLER PHRASES

NEVER use any of these:
- "Let's dive in."
- "Here's the thing..."
- "At the end of the day..."
- "In today's fast-paced digital world..."
- "Whether you're a [X] or a [Y]..."
- "More on that later."
- "But wait, there's more."

---

## 4. BANNED QUALIFIERS & HEDGING

BANNED words: "might," "could potentially," "in many cases," "often," "usually"

Replace with absolute statements backed by specific proof.
NOT "This could potentially double your leads" — USE "This exact system generated 1.4 million leads for my clients."

---

## 5. BANNED FORMATTING CRUTCHES

- **Emoji overload:** No emojis unless explicitly instructed for a specific platform. Never in VSL scripts, long-form sales pages, or email copy.
- **Excessive exclamation points:** Urgency comes from logic, offer, and proof — not punctuation.
- **Dense paragraph blocks:** No 5-6 sentence paragraphs. Line breaks are a pacing tool. Treat almost every sentence as its own line.

---

## SELF-AUDIT CHECKLIST

Before delivering ANY creative output, check:
1. Are there any parallel repetition stacks? (2+ consecutive lines with same structure) → REWRITE
2. Are there any banned phrases or transitions? → REMOVE
3. Are there any hedging qualifiers (might, could, often)? → REPLACE with specifics
4. Does every line escalate, add information, or move the argument forward? → If not, REWRITE
5. Is the rhythm varied? (mix short punchy lines with longer flowing ones) → ADJUST
