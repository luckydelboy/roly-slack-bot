3. **Barrier restoration**: Grape seed, avocado, tamanu oils + panthenol + turmeric—protective seal repair

**Key USPs**
- **Zero steroids, sulfates, synthetic fragrances**—explicitly targets medical treatment escapees
- **Multi-condition effectiveness**: Psoriasis, eczema, dandruff, product buildup—single product solves 4 problems
- **Gentle yet satisfying**: Provides scrub experience without irritation (competitors fail this balance—Christophe Robin too harsh, Briogeo too gentle)
- **30-day money-back guarantee**—risk reversal for skeptical customers
- **Results visible in 2 weeks**: 91% report significant improvement after 2 weeks

**Mechanism Differentiation**: Most treatments only mask symptoms or attack surface inflammation (Band-Aid on broken pipe). BeKynd targets root causes—scalp microbiome imbalance + broken barrier dysfunction.
